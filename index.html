Kobe Bean Bryant

The Black Mamba

1978 - 2020

At the age of 3, Kobe started to craft his connection to the game. Way back in Italy where his father played overseas, basketball became his refuge being inspired by that time's greatest through watching American basketball games.

After his decision on skipping college after high school, Kobe was drafted by the Hornets but traded to the Lakers which makes him one of the youngest players ever drafted in NBA history in which his legendary career started.

20 seasons.

5 championships.

3 Finals MVP.

2008 MVP.

17 All-star selections.

1997 slam dunk champion.

12 All-defensive team selections.

In the loving memory of a legend who changed the world of basketball and inspired a generation.
